Welcome to CyBotany!

Your go-to platform for managing and growing your plants with the help of artificial intelligence.

Key Features

Plant Identification & Health Assessment

Identify your plants and monitor their health using advanced machine learning algorithms. Our app makes it easy to detect and address potential issues.

NFC Tag Integration

Easily register and manage your plants using CyBotany tags. Our NFC tags and QR codes make it simple to track plant growth, care, and experimentation.

Customized Plant Recommendations

Get personalized plant and seed suggestions based on your preferences, location, and habits for a thriving garden.

Seed Repository

Discover and grow plants based on taste and healthiness, thanks to our extensive seed repository.

Food Safety Management

Ensure the highest food safety standards by tracking your plants and managing their growth throughout their lifecycle.

Automated Data Management

Streamline your research with automated data collection, reporting, and comparison for controlled environment plant growth facilities.

LLM Interface: CyBot

Enjoy an interactive, user-friendly experience with CyBot, our large language model interface for plant management, care, and more.
