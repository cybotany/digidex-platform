Personalize Your Digital Botanical Journey

In Digit, your experience is uniquely yours. Our user profiles allow you to customize your digital botanical journey. By adding details like your location, Digit can automatically journal local environmental variables - sunlight, humidity, temperature, and more - alongside your digitized plants.

Bridge the Gap Between Physical and Digital World

Each plant, or plant grouping, in your collection is linked using a sticker embedded with NFC technolgy, turning it into a digital touchpoint. Just a tap with your smartphone, and you're instantly connected to a digital representation, or digit, on our platform. Here you can create journal entries recording important dates, seed/plant information, or new images as needed.

Powered by Science, Perfected for Your Use

At the heart of Digit lies our commitment to precision and reliability. Our platform uses the Integrated Taxonomic Information System, a leading scientific resource for taxonomic data. Experience a new level of confidence in managing your botanical world.

Interact with Your Digitized World

Digit brings your plant data to life with our state-of-the-art AI model. Once your plants are digitized, you're not just storing data - you're unlocking a dynamic interaction. Our AI enables you to retrieve, analyze, and engage with your plants' information in real-time. Whether it's identifying plant health trends, receiving care tips, or exploring botanical insights, our platform makes every interaction intuitive.
