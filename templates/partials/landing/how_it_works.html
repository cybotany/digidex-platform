Labeling

Attach a smart sticker to any plant label. Each smart sticker has a chip with a unique identifier that links to our platform.

Digitizing

Tap the smart sticker with your smartphone to add, or digitize, a plant. The embedded chip uses contactless transfer technology to send small amounts of data to a nearby device. Like a contactless credit card, it allows users to send information by tapping it with NFC-enabled devices like most smartphones.

Managing

Access your digitized plants on our platform for easy management.
